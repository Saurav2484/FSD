Pune Transport

Buses Metro Auto Rickshaws Taxis

Buses

Pune has an extensive bus network operated by the Pune Mahanagar Parivahan Mahamandal Limited (PMPML). Buses are a cost-effective way to travel around the city.

Metro

The Pune Metro is under construction and aims to provide a fast and efficient mode of transport for Pune's residents. The metro network will connect key areas of the city.

Auto Rickshaws

Auto rickshaws are a popular mode of transport in Pune, providing flexibility and convenience for short trips.

Taxis

Taxis, including app-based services like Uber and Ola, are widely available in Pune for more comfortable and private transportation.
